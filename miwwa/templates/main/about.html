About MiWA
MiWA (Millitary Widows Association) is an initiative that strives to provide and protect the families of our fallen heroes. This organization is dedicated solely to the needs, concerns, and welfare of military surviving spouses and dependent children.
We are situated in every state in Nigeria with a headquarter in Abuja. We aim to reach out to all millitary widows to give them hope and ensure that they live a stable life after their husbands death.
Our Motto : Life Without our Husbands

Empowerment

Given the right tools, we can accomplish anything
We empower our members through vocational trainings and encourage them to start businesses with their newly attained skills.

Scholarship

We provide educational benefits for our children
Children are the leaders of tommorow and we strive to make available for our children opportunities that will give them a bright future.
